Four Ingredient Penut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup penut butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. mix together sugar, penut butter, egg, and vanilla extract
  3. scoop dough into 1 tablespoon rounds and place on lined baking sheet
  4. bake the cookies for 10 to 12 Min
  5. let the cookies cool for 5-10 minutes
