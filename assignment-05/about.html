Aesop was from Greece and he died in 564 BC, Aesop has been known for his stories about animals and for his fables known around the world today. Although he has written most of the stories there is actually evidence that many of his stories were written by several people. So, although there is no proof that he wrote the famous Fables, different storytellers worlwide have tried to recover his stories about problem solving and many other lessons, in their own languages.
