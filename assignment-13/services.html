Hitchcock resturaunt offers their own burger joint in the dining room. THey will serve burgers, sandwhiches, salads and sides. They are only offering limited dine in seating, but they also have outdoor seating just in time for summer! They have also expanded into the deli next door and it is now named Hitchcock deli.
Resturante Marche is open Thursday- Saturday and they offer limited inside dining, but they also offer dining on the patio.
Asadero is just newly opened in Ballard, with its orininal location in Kent. The Kent location did so good they felt they should open a new location for those customers up North. Aserdro's focuses on meat dishes and they are open with a full bar from 11am to 10pm. Before Covid they were offering space of up to 90 guests inside the resturant.
Cafe Munir is only open in the evenings from 5pm-8pm, whether that be to minimize contact with customers or if thats apart of their culture, it is a unique addition to Seattle. They require temperature checks and it seems they are also large on take out incuding links to uber eats and doordash on their home page.
Delancey is also a resturaunt that is only open in the evenings from 5-9 pm. They are specialized in their dinner cuisine with their pizzas and deserts. You can email them or use Tock in order to order takeout.
